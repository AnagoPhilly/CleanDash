Guides & App Installation

Go to "My Schedule". When you arrive at the job site, click the green Clock In button on your shift card. You must be physically at the location for the button to work.

You can toggle between Day, Week, and Month views using the buttons at the top of the schedule page.

GPS is required to Clock In.

iPhone (Safari)
  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security.
  2. Tap Location Services (Ensure it's ON).
  3. Find Safari Websites in the list.
  4. Select "While Using the App".
  5. Important: Turn ON "Precise Location".
Android (Chrome)
  1. Tap the Lock Icon üîí in the address bar.
  2. Tap Permissions.
  3. Tap Location and select "Allow".
  4. If hidden, check Settings > Site Settings > Location.
  5. Ensure your phone's main GPS/Location is ON.

If the account requires an alarm code, it will be displayed in red text on your shift card next to a lock icon üîí.
